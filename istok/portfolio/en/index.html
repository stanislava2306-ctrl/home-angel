How I work with brands

1

Audience research

I study the product, the market and audience behavior. I run competitor analysis and identify attention triggers and conversion points. Hypotheses come from data, not gut feeling.

2

Hypothesis testing

I create several AI concepts for different scenarios: visual style, delivery format (UGC / brand / native), engagement triggers. Then I launch tests and analyze retention, CTR and conversion.

3

AI production

I produce videos, visuals, scripts and avatars with AI tools, and optimize the content for each platform: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube.

4

Scaling

I identify the formats that work and double down on them. I build a content system: series, funnels, UGC packages — with no increase in production budget.
